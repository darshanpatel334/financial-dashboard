About This Section

Your income is what funds your financial journey. It is more than just your salary. It also includes rental income, bonuses, commissions, business income, dividends, or any side income.

Knowing how much you earn monthly or annually is crucial to decide how much you can save, invest, or spend without creating stress in your financial life.

How to go about it: List all sources of regular and irregular income. This helps you understand your cash flow and plan your saving and investing with confidence.
