About This Section

Insurance protects your wealth from risks like medical emergencies, accidents, or death. Without insurance, a single crisis can wipe out years of savings. Yet most people either don't have enough insurance or don't know how much they need.

Having the right insurance gives peace of mind and allows you to take financial decisions without fear.

How to go about it: Add your current life and health insurance policies. We will show whether your cover is adequate based on your income and family responsibilities.

Insurance Planning Guidelines

Life insurance should be 10-15x your annual income. Health insurance should cover major medical expenses. Term insurance is generally more cost-effective than endowment policies.
